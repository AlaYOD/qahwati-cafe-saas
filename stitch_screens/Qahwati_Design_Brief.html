# Qahwati (قهوتي) - SaaS POS System Design Brief ## Brand Identity - **Name:** Qahwati (Smart Management for Your Café) - **Industry:** Traditional Cafés / Coffee Shops - **Personality:** Professional, Warm, Arabic Heritage, Modern, Trustworthy ## Design System - **Colors:** Primary Browns (#4A2C2A, #2D1810), Gold Accents (#D4A574), Semantic Success/Danger/Warning colors. - **Typography:** Cairo (Google Fonts) for all text. - **Spacing:** 4px base unit. - **Components:** Rounded corners (8px-12px), Lucide Icons. ## Screens Generated 1. **Login Page:** Split screen with a warm brand visual. 2. **Dashboard:** Key metrics, sales charts, and recent activity. 3. **Tables Management:** Grid view of café tables with status indicators. 4. **POS Order Screen:** The core interface for selecting products and managing a customer's order. ## Next Steps Proposel - **Payment & Settlement:** Designing the detailed payment modal with cash and card options. - **Menu & Inventory:** Screens for adding products, tracking stock, and managing categories. - **Reporting & Analytics:** Deep-dive views for sales performance and profit margins.
